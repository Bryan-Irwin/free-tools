Ceiling Painting Tips

  • Use ceiling-specific paint, which is thicker and formulated to minimize drips and spatter.
  • Flat or matte finishes are ideal for ceilings because they hide imperfections and reduce glare.
  • Paint the ceiling before the walls to avoid drips on freshly painted surfaces below.
  • Use an extension pole with your roller to reach the ceiling comfortably without a ladder.
  • For textured ceilings (like popcorn), use a thick-nap roller (3/4 inch or more) and expect higher paint usage.
  • Work in sections, maintaining a wet edge to avoid lap marks and visible seams.
