Exterior Siding Painting Tips

  • Measure each wall of your home separately and add them together for total exterior area.
  • Subtract window and door areas from the total. A standard window is about 15 sq ft; a door is about 20 sq ft.
  • Use 100% acrylic latex exterior paint for the best durability and weather resistance.
  • Pressure-wash siding and scrape off peeling paint before applying new coats.
  • Textured siding like stucco or wood shakes absorbs more paint than smooth surfaces.
  • Avoid painting in extreme heat, direct sun, or when rain is expected within 4 hours of application.
