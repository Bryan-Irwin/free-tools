Hardwood Flooring Calculator Tips

  • Hardwood planks are typically 2.25", 3.25", or 5" wide and 12-48" long
  • Add 10% waste for straight installations, 15% for diagonal
  • Acclimate hardwood in the room for 3-5 days before installation
  • Solid hardwood can be refinished multiple times; engineered typically 1-2 times
  • Leave a 1/2" expansion gap around the perimeter of the room
