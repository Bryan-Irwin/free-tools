Floor Tile Tips

  • For straight layouts, add 10% for waste. For diagonal or herringbone, add 15-20%
  • Popular floor tile sizes are 12x12", 12x24", and 18x18"
  • Porcelain tiles are more durable than ceramic for high-traffic floor areas
  • Use larger tiles in bigger rooms to minimize grout lines and make the space feel bigger
  • Always dry-lay tiles before installing to check the pattern and plan cuts
