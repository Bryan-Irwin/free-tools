Dishwasher Tips

  • Run only full loads: Running a half-empty dishwasher wastes water and energy. Wait until it is full before starting a cycle.
  • Skip the heated dry cycle: Open the door after the wash cycle and let dishes air dry to save energy on the heating element.
  • Use the eco or energy-saving mode: Most modern dishwashers have an eco cycle that uses less water and lower temperatures.
  • Scrape, do not rinse: Modern dishwashers can handle food residue. Scraping plates instead of pre-rinsing saves both water and energy.
  • Run during off-peak hours: If your utility offers time-of-use rates, run the dishwasher at night or early morning for lower electricity costs.
  • Maintain your dishwasher: Clean the filter, spray arms, and door seal regularly to maintain optimal cleaning performance and efficiency.
