Dehumidifier Tips

  • Right-size your dehumidifier: Choose a unit rated for your room size. An undersized unit runs constantly, while an oversized one wastes energy.
  • Set an appropriate humidity level: Aim for 30-50% relative humidity. Setting the target too low forces the unit to run longer than necessary.
  • Choose an ENERGY STAR model: ENERGY STAR certified dehumidifiers use up to 15% less energy than standard models.
  • Keep doors and windows closed: Prevent humid outside air from entering the space while the dehumidifier is running.
  • Clean the filter regularly: A dirty filter reduces airflow and efficiency. Clean or replace it every few weeks.
  • Place it in the right location: Position the dehumidifier away from walls and furniture to allow proper airflow around the unit.
