LED vs Incandescent Christmas Lights

  • LED lights use up to 90% less energy: A string of 100 LED mini lights uses about 3.4 watts compared to 40 watts for incandescent mini lights.
  • LED lights last longer: LED Christmas lights can last up to 25,000 hours, while incandescent bulbs typically last around 1,000-2,000 hours.
  • LED lights run cooler: Because they generate less heat, LED lights are safer and reduce the risk of fire, especially on dry trees.
  • Use a timer: Set lights to turn on at dusk and off at bedtime to avoid running them all night and wasting electricity.
  • Solar-powered options: For outdoor displays, solar-powered lights eliminate electricity costs entirely.
  • Calculate total wattage: Check the label on each string of lights and add up the wattages for all strings to get your total wattage.
