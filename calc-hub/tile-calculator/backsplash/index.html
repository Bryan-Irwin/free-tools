Backsplash Tile Calculator Tips

  • Standard backsplash height is 18" (1.5 ft) between counter and upper cabinets
  • Subway tile (3x6") is the most popular backsplash choice
  • Add 15% waste for herringbone or chevron patterns
  • Consider using a tile edge trim instead of bullnose for a modern look
  • Measure around outlets and switches — you'll need extra tiles for these cuts
