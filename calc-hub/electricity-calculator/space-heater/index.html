Space Heater Efficiency Tips

  • Heat only the room you are in: Space heaters are most efficient when used to warm a single room rather than your entire home.
  • Choose the right size: A heater that is too powerful for a small room wastes energy. Match the heater wattage to the room size.
  • Use a thermostat model: Heaters with built-in thermostats automatically cycle off when the desired temperature is reached, saving energy.
  • Seal drafts: Close windows and doors, and seal gaps to prevent warm air from escaping the room.
  • Use a timer: Set a timer to turn off the heater when you leave or go to sleep to avoid running it unnecessarily.
  • Consider a ceramic heater: Ceramic space heaters heat up quickly and retain heat well, making them more energy-efficient than older coil models.
